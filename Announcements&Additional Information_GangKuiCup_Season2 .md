-   No VRS filter and no VRS invitations; all participants will be invited through open registration on a "first-come-first-served" basis.

-   The event is open to teams from the Global Region, as defined in Valve's official rules & regulations.

-   The registration fee per team:500 USD.

     -    Re-fund policy: If your team needs to withdraw from the tournament before March 18(Beijing Time) we will try to replace your team with teams on the waiting list. If we find a replacement we will re-fund the full amount. If teams withdraw or fail to lock in lineup after March 18(Beijing Time) no refund will be made.

-   Initial seeding will be determined according to the VRS list as of March 2026.

-   Our event aligns with Valve’s Ranked Tier 2 requirements. While Tier 2 events may use direct VRS invitations, GangKui Cup Season2 will not issue direct invites. Instead, the top 4 registered teams by VRS will be directly seeded into the Main Event（Playoffs）.

## Play-In

-   This stage will be held online

-   Format: Four round-robin format groups

    -    Each group has 5 teams

    -    All matches are Bo1

    -    The top 2 teams from each group advance to the Group Stage

## Group Stage

-   This stage will be held online.

-   Format: Two double-elimination format Groups

    -    Each group has 4 teams

    -    Opening and Winners' matches are Bo1

    -    Elimination and Decider matches are Bo3

    -    Top two teams from each group advance to the Playoffs

## Playoffs

-   This stage will be held offline.

-   Format: Double-Elimination bracket.

    -    All matches are Bo3.  

## Event Location：Shanghai, China

## Prize Distribution
